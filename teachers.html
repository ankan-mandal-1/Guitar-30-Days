Frequently Asked Questions

1. I have never played guitar before. Can I really learn in 6-12 months?

Yes! Most beginners start playing their first songs within about 3 months of regular practice. Everyone learns differently.

2. Do I need an expensive guitar to start?

No! You can start with any basic acoustic or electric guitar. We also provide recommendations for budget-friendly options.

3. How much time do I need to practice daily?

Even 20-40 minutes a day can help you make steady progress. The class is designed to fit into your busy schedule.
