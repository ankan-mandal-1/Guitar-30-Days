Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own guitar? expand_more

Yes, you will need an acoustic or electric guitar to participate in the class. If you don't have one yet, we can recommend some great beginner options!

How much time do I need to practice daily? expand_more

Even 20 minutes a day can help you make steady progress. The syllabus is designed to fit into your busy schedule.

Is this class really for absolute beginners? expand_more

Absolutely! This class is designed from the ground up for individuals with zero prior guitar experience. We'll start with the very basics.
