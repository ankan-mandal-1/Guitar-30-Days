Frequently Asked Questions

I've never played ukulele before — can I really learn in 30 days? +
Yes! Most beginners start playing their first songs within about 30 days of regular practice. Everyone learns at a different pace, which is why this course is fully self-paced.
Do I need an expensive ukulele to start? +
Not at all. Any basic acoustic ukulele works. We also provide recommendations for budget-friendly options so you can get started without overspending.
How much time do I need to practice each day? +
Even 20 minutes a day is enough to make steady progress. The course is designed to fit into busy schedules — consistency matters more than duration.
